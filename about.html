Our Mission

More Than Just Gutters

After serving our country, we wanted to continue serving our community. We noticed a lack of reliability in the local trades—contractors who didn't show up, didn't communicate, or cut corners.


We founded Veteran Gutters & Guards to change that narrative. We believe that protecting your home shouldn't be stressful. It should be a positive experience, handled by professionals who respect your time, your property, and your trust.

Integrity First

We do what we say we're going to do. If we quote a price, that's the price. If we set a time, we're there.

Precision Craftsmanship

In the military, details matter. We apply that same scrutiny to every seamless joint and every hanger we install.

Positive Impact

We bring positivity to every job site. We love what we do, and we want you to feel good about your investment.

Community Focused

We don't just work here; we live here. We're proud to serve our neighbors in Ocala, The Villages, and beyond.
