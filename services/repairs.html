Frequently Asked Questions

Can you repair just one section of gutter?

Yes! We don't force you to buy a whole new system. If a single run is damaged by a tree branch or accident, we can replace just that section to match.

Why are my gutters pulling away from the house?

This is usually caused by rotting fascia board or loose spikes. We replace old spikes with heavy-duty hidden screw hangers that bite deep into solid wood.

Do you fix leaking corners?

Yes. Leaking corners (miters) are the most common repair we do. We strip the old sealant and apply a new industrial-grade geo-polymer sealant that lasts for years.

Is there a minimum charge for repairs?

We have a standard service call minimum to cover travel and setup, but we always visually inspect your entire system while we are there.
