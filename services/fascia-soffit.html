Go Beyond the Gutter

Gutters hanging off rotten wood is a recipe for disaster. If your fascia board is soft or damaged, new gutters won't hold.

We specialize in full structural repair. We remove the rot, replace the lumber, and wrap it in aluminum (capping) so you never have to paint it again.

  • Rotten Wood Replacement: Pressure-treated lumber installation.
  • Vinyl Soffit Venting: Improves attic airflow and reduces heat.
  • Aluminum Capping: Maintenance-free protection for wood trim.

Our Restoration Process

01.

Remove & Inspect

We carefully take down existing gutters and peel back the fascia to expose hidden rot and termite damage.

02.

Structural Repair

We cut out damaged sections and install fresh, pressure-treated lumber to restore the roofline's load-bearing capacity.

03.

Finish & Install

We install vented soffit panels and wrap the fascia in aluminum trim, providing a clean surface for new gutters.
